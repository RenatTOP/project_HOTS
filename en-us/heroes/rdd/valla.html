Valla

Demon Hunter

Valla witnessed the ferocity of hellspawn firsthand as the demons ravaged her village and left her for dead. Now, with no people to call her own, her only allegiance is to her cause: to rid Sanctuary of the demonic filth corrupting its lands.

  • Ranged Assassin
  • Уровень: 0

  • Hungering Arrow

    Fire an arrow that deals 140 damage to the first target hit, then seeks up to 2 additional enemies, prioritizing Heroes, dealing 80 damage. Can hit an enemy multiple times.

  • Multishot

    Deal 172 damage to enemies within the target area.

  • Vault

    Dash to the target area. Valla's next Basic Attack within 2 seconds deals 6% increased damage per stack of Hatred.

  • Strafe

    Rapidly attack enemies within 10 range for 60 damage per hit, prioritizing Heroes over Minions. Valla is able to move and use Vault while strafing. Lasts for 4 seconds.

  • Rain of Vengeance

    Launch a wave of Shadow Beasts that deals 250 damage and stuns enemies in the target area for 0.5 seconds. Stores up to 2 charges.

  • Hatred

    Basic Attacks grant a stack of Hatred, up to 10. Each Hatred stack increases Basic Attack damage by 8% and Movement Speed by 1%. Lasts 6 seconds.
