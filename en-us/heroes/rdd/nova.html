Nova

Dominion Ghost

November "Nova" Terra is a ghost of the Terran Dominion and one of the most powerful psionic soldiers ever known. Nova is highly focused, determined, and extremely deadly. Even Jim Raynor wouldn't want to be caught in her crosshairs.

  • Ranged Assassin

  • Snipe

    Deal 230 damage to the first enemy hit. Passive: Hitting an enemy Hero with Snipe permanently increases the damage of Snipe by 6%, stacking up to 30%. Gain an additional 25% damage bonus at maximum stacks. All stacks are lost if Snipe fails to hit an enemy.

  • Pinning Shot

    Deal 100 damage to an enemy and Slow them by 40% for 2.25 seconds.

  • Holo Decoy

    Create a Decoy for 5 seconds with 100% of Nova's current Health that attacks enemies, dealing 10% of Nova's base damage. Whenever a Decoy takes damage, it deals that amount of damage to itself, effectively doubling the damage it takes. Using this Ability does not break Stealth.

  • Ghost Protocol

    Activate to instantly grant Stealth to Nova and spawn a Holo Decoy at her location. Nova is Unrevealable for the first 0.5 seconds when Stealthed by Ghost Protocol.

  • Triple Tap

    Locks in on the target Hero, then fires 3 shots that hit the first Hero or Structure they come in contact with for 372 damage each.

  • Precision Strike

    After a 1.5 second delay, deals 435 damage to enemies within an area. Unlimited range.

  • Permanent Cloak

    Gain Stealth when out of combat for 3 seconds. Taking damage, attacking, using Abilities, or Channeling ends Stealth. Remaining stationary for at least 1.5 seconds while Stealthed grants Invisible. Passive: Gain 15% Movement Speed while Stealthed.
