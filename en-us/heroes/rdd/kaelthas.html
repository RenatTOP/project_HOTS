  • Heroic Abilities
  • Primary Abilities
  • Trait

  • Flamestrike

    After 1 second, deal 345 damage in an area. Verdant Spheres increases the radius by 50%.

  • Living Bomb

    Deal 126 damage over 3 seconds to an enemy, then they explode dealing 215 damage to all nearby enemies. Other Heroes damaged by this explosion are also affected by Living Bomb, though the secondary explosions cannot spread. Verdant Spheres makes this Ability cost no Mana and have no cooldown.

  • Gravity Lapse

    Stun the first enemy hit for 1 second. Verdant Spheres causes Gravity Lapse to stun the first 3 enemies hit and increases the stun duration by 50%.

  • Phoenix

    Launch a Phoenix to an area, dealing 78 damage to enemies along the way. The Phoenix persists for 7 seconds, attacking enemies for 78 damage and splashing for 39 damage.

  • Pyroblast

    After 1.5 seconds, cast a slow-moving fireball that deals 810 damage to an enemy Hero and 405 damage to enemies nearby.

  • Verdant Spheres

    Activate to make Kael'thas's next Basic Ability more powerful.
