Nazeebo

Heretic Witch Doctor

  • Heroic Abilities
  • Primary Abilities
  • Trait

  • Corpse Spiders

    Hurl a jar of spiders that deals 50 damage. If it hits at least one enemy, create 3 Corpse Spiders that attack for 38 damage. Spiders last for 4 seconds.

  • Zombie Wall

    After 1 second, create a ring of Zombies surrounding the target area that deal 28 damage and last for 3 seconds.

  • Plague of Toads

    Create a wave of 3 Toads that explode on contact, dealing 119 damage over 6 seconds. This effect stacks. Stores up to 2 charges.

  • Gargantuan

    Summon a Gargantuan to guard an area for 20 seconds. Deals 100 damage when summoned, attacks for 140 damage, and can be ordered to stomp nearby enemies.

  • Ravenous Spirit

    Channel a Ravenous Spirit that deals 230 damage per second. Cannot move while channeling. Lasts for 8 seconds.

  • Voodoo Ritual

    Nazeebo's Basic Attacks and Abilities poison Non-Heroic enemies, causing them to take 67 additional damage over 6 seconds. Quest: If a Minion dies while poisoned by Voodoo Ritual, Nazeebo permanently gains 6 Health and 1 Mana.
