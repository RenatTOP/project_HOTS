Anub'arak

Traitor King

Once a proud nerubian king, Anub'arak dared to stand against the Lich King's invasion, but despite his bravery, he too fell to the unrelenting Scourge. Resurrected into undeath, the Traitor King now serves as a towering weapon of destruction.

  • Tank

  • Impale

    Deals 90 damage. Stuns for 1 second.

  • Harden Carapace

    Gain a Shield that grants 40 Spell Armor and absorbs 315 damage over 3 seconds.

  • Burrow Charge

    Burrow to the target location, dealing 91 damage and briefly stunning enemies in a small area upon surfacing, slowing them by 25% for 2.5 seconds. Burrow Charge can be reactivated to surface early.

  • Locust Swarm

    Deal 68 damage per second to nearby enemies. Each enemy damaged restores 24 Health. Lasts 6 seconds.

  • Cocoon

    Wraps target enemy Hero in a cocoon, rendering them unable to act or be targeted for 7 seconds. Allies of the Hero can attack the cocoon to break it and free them early.

  • Scarab Host

    Using an Ability spawns a Beetle which lasts for 8 seconds, attacking nearby enemies for 20 damage.
