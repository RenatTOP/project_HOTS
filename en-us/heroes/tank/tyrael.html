Tyrael

Archangel of Justice

Among the angels it is the Archangel of Justice Tyrael who is humanity's greatest defender. Wielding his sword El'druin against the Burning Hells, the Prime Evils would have enslaved humanity long ago if Tyrael had not intervened.

  • Heroic Abilities
  • Primary Abilities
  • Trait

  • El'druin's Might

    Throw El'druin to the target area, dealing 110 damage to nearby enemies and Slowing them by 25% for 2.5 seconds. It can be reactivated within 5 seconds to teleport Tyrael to El'druin and Slow nearby enemies again.

  • Righteousness

    Shields Tyrael for 336 damage and nearby allied Heroes and Minions for 40% as much for 4 seconds.

  • Smite

    Rake target area for 150 damage. Allies moving through the targeted area gain 25% increased Movement Speed for 2 seconds.

  • Judgment

    After 0.75 seconds, charge an enemy Hero, dealing 150 damage and Stunning them for 1.5 seconds. Nearby enemies are knocked away and take 75 damage.

  • Sanctification

    After 0.5 seconds create a field of holy energy that makes allied Heroes Invulnerable. Lasts 3 seconds.

  • Archangel's Wrath

    Upon dying, become Invulnerable and explode for 550 damage after 3.5 seconds. Each enemy Hero hit by this effect reduces Tyrael's death timer by 10%.
