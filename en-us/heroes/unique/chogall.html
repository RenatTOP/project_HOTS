  • Tank
  • Ranged Assassin

  • Surging Fist

    Activate to begin charging Surging Fist. Activate again to dash forward, knocking aside enemies and dealing 46 damage. The dash range increases by up to 250%, depending on how long it is charged.

  • Shadowflame

    Deal 135 damage to enemies in the area.

  • Consuming Blaze

    Ignite nearby enemies, dealing 150 damage over 5 seconds. Basic Attacking burning enemies re-Ignites them. Cho is healed for 40 when an enemy is Ignited.

  • Dread Orb

    Throw a bomb that will bounce three times, dealing 126 damage to enemies.

  • Rune Bomb

    Roll a bomb dealing 91 damage to enemies in its path. Gall can use Runic Blast to detonate it to deal 210 damage in an area.

  • Runic Blast

    Detonate Cho's Rune Bomb, dealing 210 damage around it.

  • Hammer of Twilight

    Activate to swing the Hammer of Twilight, dealing 150 damage, pushing enemies away, and Stunning them for 0.75 seconds. Passive: Cho's Basic Attacks deal 25% increased damage.

  • Shadow Bolt Volley

    After 1 second, unleash 20 Shadow Bolts over 4 seconds, each dealing 87 damage to the first target hit. The bolts fire towards your mouse.

  • Upheaval

    After 1 second, pull enemies towards Cho'gall, slowing them by 25% for 3 seconds and dealing 175 damage.

  • Twisting Nether

    After 1 second, nearby enemies are slowed by 50% while Gall channels, up to 5 seconds. Activate to deal 353 damage.

  • Ogre Hide

    Activate to gain 25 Armor, but reduce Gall's damage by 25%.

  • Ogre Rage

    Activate to increase Gall's damage by 25%, but reduce Cho's Armor by 25. Passive: Gall is permanently immune to Stun and Silence effects.
