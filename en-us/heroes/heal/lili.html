Li Li

World Wanderer

Born upon Shen-zin Su, the Wandering Isle, Li Li Stormstout led an uneventful life until she began reading her uncle Chen's journals. Soon, nothing would sate her desire for adventure, exploration, and pestering adventurers around Halfhill.

  • Primary Abilities
  • Trait

  • Healing Brew

    Heal lowest Health ally (prioritizing Heroes) within 6 range for 175 Health.

  • Cloud Serpent

    Summon a Cloud Serpent on an allied Hero that attacks nearby enemies every second. Each attack deals 26 damage and heals the ally for 20. Lasts for 8 seconds.

  • Blinding Wind

    Throw a cloud of wind at the 2 nearest enemies (prioritizing Heroes), dealing 133 damage. Affected targets are Blinded for 1.5 seconds, causing their Basic Attacks to miss and deal no damage.

  • Jug of 1,000 Cups

    Channel for up to 6 seconds. Every 0.25 seconds, heal the lowest Health nearby allied Hero for 66 Health and increase the cooldown of Jug of 1,000 Cups by 2 seconds, up to 50.

  • Water Dragon

    Li Li channels for 2 seconds, summoning a Water Dragon that hits the nearest enemy Hero within 12 range and all enemies near them, dealing 300 damage and slowing their Movement Speed by 70% for 4 seconds.

  • Fast Feet

    Upon taking damage, your Basic Ability cooldowns refresh 50% faster and you gain 10% Movement Speed for 1 second.
