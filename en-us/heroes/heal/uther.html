Uther

The Lightbringer

The first paladin and founder of the Order of the Silver Hand, Uther the Lightbringer is a faithful servant of the Light. Dispensing justice with the swing of his hammer, Uther is a bastion of truth and an unflinching symbol of the Alliance.

  • Healer

  • Holy Light

    Heal target allied Hero for 362 Health. When used on a target other than Uther, also heal Uther for 181 Health.

  • Holy Radiance

    Heal all allied Heroes and Minions in a line for 177 Health, dealing 177 damage to enemies.

  • Hammer of Justice

    Deal 109 damage and Stun the target for 1 second.

  • Divine Shield

    Make an allied Hero Invulnerable and increase their Movement Speed by 20% for 3 seconds.

  • Divine Storm

    Deal 170 damage and Stun nearby enemies for 1.75 seconds.

  • Devotion, Eternal Vanguard

    Allied Heroes affected by Uther's Basic Abilities gain 25 Armor for 2 seconds. This effect does not stack with itself. Upon dying, Uther becomes an Invulnerable spirit for up to 8 seconds. While in spirit form, Uther can heal allies with Flash of Light.
