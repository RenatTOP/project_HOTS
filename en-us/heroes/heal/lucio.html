  • Heroic Abilities
  • Primary Abilities
  • Trait

  • Soundwave

    Deal 105 damage to enemies in an area and knock them back.

  • Crossfade: Speed Boost

    Passively increase the Movement Speed of Lúcio and nearby allied Heroes by 15%. Toggle to play Healing Boost instead.

  • Crossfade: Healing Boost

    Passively heal Lúcio and nearby allied Heroes for 25 health per second. Toggle to play Speed Boost instead.

  • Amp It Up

    Raise Lúcio's Crossfade track volume for 3 seconds, amping Healing Boost to 114 health per second and Speed Boost to 45% increased Movement Speed.

  • Wall Ride

    When moving alongside terrain, Lúcio begins to Wall Ride for 2 seconds. While Wall Ride is active, Lúcio can walk through units and gains 20% Movement Speed that stacks with other bonuses.

  • Sound Barrier

    After 1 second, Lúcio and nearby allied Heroes gain a 1296 point Shield that rapidly decays over 6 seconds.

  • High Five

    Quickly skate to an allied Hero. Upon arrival, the ally is granted Unstoppable for 1 second and is healed for 250.

  • Push Off

    While moving alongside terrain, activate to slide towards a targeted location. Enemies hit take 100 damage and are Slowed by 75% for 1 second.
