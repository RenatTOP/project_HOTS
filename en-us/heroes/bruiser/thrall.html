Thrall

Warchief of the Horde

As a child, Thrall served the cruel Aedelas Blackmoore as a slave. After gaining his freedom, he guided the liberated orcs to a land of their own and worked to recover their ancient traditions. Now and forever he is the Warchief of the Horde.

  • Bruiser

  • Chain Lightning

    Shock an enemy with lightning, dealing 162 damage. The lightning then bounces 3 times to nearby enemies, dealing 81 damage to each enemy hit.

  • Feral Spirit

    Unleash a Feral Spirit that deals 153 damage to enemies in its path and Roots Heroes hit for 1 second. Each Hero hit increases the distance traveled by 25%.

  • Windfury

    Increase Thrall's Movement Speed by 30% for 4 seconds. His next 3 Basic Attacks occur 100% faster and generate stacks of Frostwolf Resilience.

  • Sundering

    After 0.5 seconds, sunder the earth in a long line, dealing 290 damage and shoving enemies to the side, Stunning them for 1 second.

  • Earthquake

    After 0.5 seconds, summon a massive Earthquake that pulses every 4 seconds. Each pulse lasts 2 seconds, Slowing all enemies in the area by 50%, and deals 50 damage to enemy Heroes. Does 3 pulses.

  • Frostwolf Resilience

    Dealing damage with Abilities grants 1 stack of Frostwolf Resilience. At 5 stacks, Thrall is instantly healed for 223 Health.
