  • Support

  • Select Olaf

    Olaf can charge enemies and slow their Movement Speed by 30% for 3 seconds by right-clicking on them. 8 second cooldown. Olaf gains increased Health Regeneration when out of combat for 4 seconds.

  • Select Baleog

    Baleog's Basic Attacks deal 50% splash damage to enemies behind his attack target.

  • Select Erik

    Erik moves faster than the other Vikings and has increased Basic Attack range.

  • Longboat Raid!

    Hop into an Unstoppable Longboat that fires at nearby enemies for 112 damage per second and can fire a mortar that deals 228 damage in an area. The boat has increased Health for each Viking inside. If the boat is destroyed by enemies, all Vikings are Stunned for 1 second. Lasts 15 seconds. Requires all surviving Vikings to be nearby.

  • Play Again!

    Summon, fully heal, and revive all Lost Vikings at target location after a Viking channels for 2 seconds. Only one Viking may attempt to summon at a time.

  • Viking Hoard

    Gathering a Regeneration Globe with a Viking permanently increases all their Health Regeneration by 0.5 per second.
