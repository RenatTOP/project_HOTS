Battlegrounds

Alterac Pass

A long-standing feud broke out with renewed vigor: the Horde and the Alliance clashed in a battle over a strategic pass through the Alterac Mountains. The generals of each side collect heroes under their own banners, trying to defeat the enemy and capture the Alterac Pass.
Capture Prison Camps and unleash Cavalry to destroy the enemy General.

Towers of Doom

The Grave Digger and Lord of the Ravens have come together in an eternal confrontation with evil... ...to another evil?
Control the Bell Towers, capture the Altars, and break through the enemy Core’s impenetrable shield.

Braxis Holdout

On a distant and highly hostile planet, the Braxis United Earth Directorate has in the past conducted experiments designed to turn the Zerg Roy into a weapon. Although the main psi annulator has been destroyed, beacons on the orbital platform can still be used.
Capture the beacons and watch what happens when a flood of weaponized Zerg ravages the battlefield!

Blackheart's Bay

The ghost pirate Chernoserd has declared himself Lord of the Fog Harbour. Members of his dissolute crew of undead have seeped into the streets, turning a once thriving center of commerce into a monastery of vice and robbery.
Collect doubloons and pay the ghost pirate Blackheart to turn his guns on your enemies. If you see a cannonball flying your way… run!

Battlefield of Eternity

Heaven and hell collided (literally) on the battlefield, symbolizing their eternal confrontation
Two Immortals are locked in a duel to the death. Help your Immortal prevail, and he’ll devastate your enemy’s forts.

Tomb of the Spider Queen

Deep beneath the crumbling vaults of the forgotten tombs of Luxoria, the queen of spiders Neithis awakened from sleep.
Slay your enemies and gather gems of magical power. Bring enough to the Spider Queen’s altar, and her skittering Webweavers will devour your foes.

Dragon Shire

Цветущие, безмятежные, на первый взгляд, сады Драконьего края выросли на просторах раскаленной пустыни, некогда именуемой Драконьим шпилем.
He who controls the shrines controls the dragon; he who controls the dragon punts the other team’s puny heroes.

Volskaya Foundry

The heroes of the Nexus rushed to the Volskaya factory. Capture the control of giant robots and destroy your enemies with them!
Capture control points to unlock a Protector mech controlled by two players at once.

Sky Temple

The celestial temple, which soars in the heights above the endless sands of Luxoria, serves as a center of worship for the serpent god Ka.
Capture the ancient temples, defeat their guardians, and use their awesome power to destroy your enemy’s forts.

Infernal Shrines

A dark, destructive force threatens the Gardens of Hope, creatures made of equal parts mayhem and destruction. Harness their devastating might, or perish at the hands of the Punishers.

Haunted Mines

Away from the damned ravine, at the very edge of the Crow's Court, there are Ghost Mines. Undead, caught in the tunnels beneath a mysterious cemetery, relentlessly trying to break free from of his afterlife prison.

Cursed Hollow

The Crow Yard is one of the oldest worlds consumed by the Nexus. Over the millennia, it has grown and prospered.
It really is a horrible night to have a curse. Gather the Raven Lord’s tribute and make sure it’s the other team and not yours that has to suffer.

Garden of Terror

Once the royal flower gardens were a spectacular sight, but then they were swallowed up by the darkness. Slay the Shamblers, summon Terrors in every lane, and follow your green thumb to victory!

Hanamura Temple

The Battle of the Nexus came to Hanamura, the Shimada clan's ancestral estate. Will you be able to curb the power of dragons and lead your team to victory?
On the temple’s grounds, escort the payload to its destination and bombard your enemy!

Warhead Junction

Previously, the nuclear test site was used to test powerful weapons, but it was abandoned long ago. However, due to a software failure, the base aide continues to produce nuclear weapons and give them to anyone who wants them.
Join the arms race of Warhead Junction, build up your stockpile, and rain hell on your enemies!
