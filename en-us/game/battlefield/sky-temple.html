Sky Temple

Map Objectives

  • Capture the Temples

    Temples will periodically activate. Stand within their grounds to capture their power!

  • Hold the Temples

    Stand firm and the Temple will unleash a blistering onslaught on your enemy's forts!

  • Defend the Temples

    Guardians will try to wrest control of their Temples from your team. Hold them off to keep the Temple's power for yourselves!
