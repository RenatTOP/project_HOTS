Haunted Mines

Map Objectives

  • Venture Underground

    The Haunted Mines will periodically open with an Undead Army underneath!

    Destroy the Undead and collect their Skulls to power your Golems!

  • Unleash the Golems

    After the Army has been defeated, the Golems will arise for both teams! The more Skulls your team collects, the stronger your Golem!
